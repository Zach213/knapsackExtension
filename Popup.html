Welcome to knapsackOptimize!

Why did I make this?

Oftentimes, we have a ton of things that we need to do but it's hard to choose between them. By quantifying the value and time that each will take, you can better choose what will give you the most bang for your buck. Moreso, by using the Knapsack algorithm, you'll get the mathematically optimal solution, so no more second-guessing yourself. Think of this like the "Find best word" in WordsWithFriends or Scrabble!

Let's optimize in 3 steps!

  • Step 1:

    Put in how much time you have.

  • Step 2:

    Put in each activity with its value and time it'll take.

  • Step 3:

    Press submit and see your optimized suggestion done using the Knapsack algorithm!
